Profile

From Frappe Forum regular to product engineer, without ever leaving the community

Nihantra builds and supports Frappe Lending, an open source lending product on the Frappe Framework. Long before that title existed, he was already a familiar name on the Frappe Forum, answering questions for anyone who asked, for years, with no obligation to do so.

That habit turned into a career in 2024, and he still spends part of every week back on the forum today.
